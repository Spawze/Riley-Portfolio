About Me

Hello! My name is Robert Riley Allred,(he/him) and I'm years old. I was given the same name as my dad, Robert, so I go by my middle name, Riley. I was born in Utah. My favorite color is green (if you couldn't already tell). My interest in computers started when I was young, and took a lot of interest in coding around when I was in highschool. After highschool I loosely taught myself some things, but I've learned most during the UofU web development boot-camp.

My hobbies include: art, gaming, watching video or movies, furry fandom, and hanging out with friends. Currently I work at the Associated Food Stores warehouse in Ogden UT, building pallets of produce, and deli products, to provide for local grocery stores.

I am proudly part of the LGBTQ+ community. The character above with sunglasses is my online persona that I created, named Spawze. I usually use him as an avatar and username to represent myself online, since I'd rather not use my real name if I don't have to.
